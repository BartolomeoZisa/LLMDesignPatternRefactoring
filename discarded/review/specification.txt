Customer Reviews for Merchant:

Requirement: Customer reviews must include a rating in tenths.

Optional: One or more comments can be provided for the commission and/or for the merchant.

Objective:

The Review Interface must provide the method:
String getDescription(....)

The string returned should:

Contain the rating and the merchant's comments.

If the requester is a member of the commission, it should also include commission comments.
